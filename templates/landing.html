How It Works

From Login to Secure Completion

A seamless 4-step process that keeps exams honest from start to finish.

1
Register & Identity Recording

Create your account and record a 15-second video+audio baseline. This is your identity fingerprint for the session.

2
Grant Camera & Mic Permissions

Allow browser access to your camera and microphone. DeepGuard AI needs these to monitor your session in real time.

3
Enter Fullscreen & Begin Exam

Questions appear one at a time. AI continuously verifies your identity, checks for extra persons, and monitors for deepfakes.

4
Submit & View Results

Submit when ready (or auto-submit on timer end). Your results and session report are available on the dashboard.
